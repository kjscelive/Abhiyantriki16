DR. Bikash Chandra Sinha

Dr Bikash Sinha, an expert in the field of nuclear physics, was the chairman of the board of directors at NIT, Durgapur. He was also a member of scientific advisory board to the Prime Minister of India. He was awarded the Padma Shri in 2001 and Padma Bhushan in 2010. He was previously the director of Saha Institute of Nuclear Physics and Homi Bhabha Chair Professor of the Variable Electron Cyclotron Centre. Dr Sinha will speak on the topics- Cosmology, Mini Bang between two nuclei and the Big Bang.
